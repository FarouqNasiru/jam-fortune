About Us


We are one of the strongest Nigeria's private and corporate financial institution, popularly known as JAM-FORTUNE FINANCIAL SERVICES LTD, a fully established Micro-finance Institution, being incorporated by the federal government with licence to run a credit facilities scheme, purposely to enhance the phases of investment and small scale business growth for Nigerian citizens without any tribal, or religious discrimination across the states of the federation.
The corporate financial institute since inception in 2008, has an integrity financial board stakeholders, who has an heart feeling about the deteriorating level of poverty in the Nigeria society and laced with the resolution to eradicate this financial burden for many lacking individuals and with their good spirit of financial supportive patronage to the financial institute; embellished with core professional experience which has robust hundred of our beneficiary in various easier trade for wealthy life.
Hence, the strong bond between us and our customers will continue to reduce and totally eradicate poverty and un-employment in Nigeria.
In a nutshell and worthy of note, Jam-Fortune Financial Institute Ltd, is a member of Association of Non-bank Micro-Finance Institution of Nigeria (ANMFIN).
